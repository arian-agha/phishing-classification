Phishing Email Classification - Dashboard

There will be five phishing and five legitimate emails in an undisclosed order, and your task is to classify them correctly.

There will be five sets, each containing two emails. You have 12 seconds to view each email.

After every set, you will be asked to make your classification for both emails that you were shown.

Before every set, a "gaze calibration" will be performed through your webcam, which is a simple 30-second process.

Only anonymised gaze data with the corresponding classifications are stored. No other data is stored.

The process should take about 10 minutes in total.
